Welcome!

Raz Clemente is a driven competitor specializing in several industries. Clemente grew up aspiring to be a professional tennis player while maintaining a strong passion for digital infrastructure. Throughout his tennis career he maintained a top ranking in multiple FPS IPs. After desiring more out of life besides tennis, Clemente began to pursue a career in software development. Given his ever present fascination in the gaming industry, Clemente believes he can play an instrumental role in progressing the industry. What gives him a unique edge in the field is his complete lack of monetary value. He cares solely for the passion of the community and the betterment of the industry.
